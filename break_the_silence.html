Learn — Signs & Prevention

What grooming looks like

Grooming is when someone tries to gain a kid's trust to manipulate them. It can happen online or in person, usually starts with being really nice or giving attention, then slowly escalates.

  • Secretive conversations or friendships
  • Asking for personal info or pictures
  • Rushing intimacy, gifts, or favors
  • Pressuring you to keep things secret

How to stay safe

  • Use strong privacy settings and don’t share your location.
  • Be careful talking to strangers online — double-check who they are.
  • Tell a trusted adult if something feels off. You won’t get in trouble.
  • Save screenshots or messages if you need proof, and reach out to hotlines if someone’s in danger.
